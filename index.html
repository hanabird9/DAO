Our Brand Story

Traditional Charcoal BBQ Heritage

Welcome to Daorae Korean BBQ in Bukit Indah. As a premier destination for traditional Korean dining in Malaysia, we are committed to keeping the spirit of authentic food alive. We grill exclusively over natural wood charcoal, allowing the smoky aroma to infuse into every slice of our premium beef and pork.

At Daorae, dining is an experience of warmth and sharing. Our tables are equipped with high-tech copper ventilation pipes, allowing you to enjoy the authentic, interactive tabletop grill experience in complete comfort and style.

Natural Charcoal Grills

We never use gas plates. Natural charcoal imparts an irreplaceable smoky flavor.
